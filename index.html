    About

    Target Tech for Techgirls is a bootcamp for girls who are interested in learning software development. It is a platform to meet other techgirls who have the same goal and ambitions, just like you. Get trained with your variety of software development skills such as UI/UX designs, Front-end development and Back-end development.You don't need a knowledge of computers to get started with us, as we will take you right from the grassroot level to the very top.

    Fully Practicals

    All hands on practical learning and weekly cloning challenges to expose you to a real live project.

    What you need

    All you need is a good network and your laptop. Of course, your commitment and focus will drive you a long way.

    Mentorship

    Direct access to competent and experienced mentors in the tech community to guide you through your learning process as well as to direct you to the right path as you kickstart your tech career.

    Our services

    At our Bootcamp, we offer courses on;

    • UI/UX Designs
    • Front-end Development
    • Back-end Development
    • Graphics Designing
    • Cryptocurrency
    • Mentorship Programs
